Who we are

At Scholarship Skillz we are dedicated to helping basketball players of any skill level reach their full potential. We are a team of professional skills trainers, based in Central Florida. Our program aims to develop and refine skills such as shooting, passing, footwork, and ball-handling. Through our training methodology we build our player's confidence and understanding of the game. We pride ourselves in helping you succeed at the game we love.
